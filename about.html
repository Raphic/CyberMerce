About Us

Cyber means information technology (IT) Merce means commercial business. It’s everything we do.
We focus on three main areas: 1. IT Solutions, 2. IT Professional Services, 3. IT Care and Supports.

CYBERMERCE is formerly known as DMS (Digital Marketing Solutions LLC)


Digital Marketing Solutions LLC has taken on a new name. Why? Well, let me begin with the quote by Albert Einstein, “The measure of intelligence is the ability to change.”


Our history started out in early 1995 when we began our business as Christian Brothers Home Computer Solutions providing home computer training and set up for residents who found computers confusing and frustrating. In June of 2003, we changed our name to Digital Marketing Solution when our business transitioned to helping customers create websites using a notepad and basic HTML. Over the years, our company has grown and morphed into specialized areas of technology and applications and the name Digital Marketing Solutions no longer fits what we do. We are often being confused and associated with many of the online “marketing” companies out there. The thought of changing our name, discarding all of our marketing materials and having to re-brand ourselves again, is agonizing to say the least. We sat on this decision for more than three years and finally, in June of 2019, we have decided to move forward with the change for the sake of the future of our company.


Digital Marketing Solutions LLC is now CyberMerce LLC. Cyber means information technology (IT) Merce means commercial business. It’s everything we do. We focus on three main areas: 1. IT Solutions, 2. IT Professional Services, 3. IT Care and Supports.
